Description:

Distinctive appearance. Red head/throrax, pale legs, dark bluish-black forewings. Last segment of palp is black and oval flattened shape. Female forewings are convex similar to beetles. Song (of male) is a "rattling, broken trill", given both day and night. Left wing of male is clear, apparently modified for stridulation.
